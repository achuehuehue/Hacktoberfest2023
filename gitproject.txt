The INSTITUTION OF ELECTRONICS AND TELECOMMUNICATION ENGINEERING is a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts, innovators and learners.We are dedicated to fostering a passion for technology and encouraging creativity in digital realm. IETE organizes tech talks and workshops where we learn about cutting-edge technologies,trends and carrier opportunities in the tech world.So whether you are a begginer, an intermediate learner or an experienced tech professional, the club welcomes everyone.

Hacktoberfest is an annual event that encourages developers, designers and tech enthusiasts from around the globe to contribute to open source projects throughout the month of October.It's a fantastic opportunity to learn, collaborate,and give back to the community that powers the digital world.IETE is involved in hacktoberfest to give a chance to students to be involved in open source.
